Bio


I am a Ph.D. student in the MS&E Department at Stanford University. Prior to Stanford, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at Sharif University of Technology.
My research is mostly in design and analysis of algorithms, operations research and optimization. Throughout my Ph.D. I have interned in several research labs including Google Research, and Microsoft Research. You can find my resume here for more information.
